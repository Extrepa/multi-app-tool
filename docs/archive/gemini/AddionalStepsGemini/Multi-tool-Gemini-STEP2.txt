### **2. Key UI Interactions**

* **The Surgical Border:** Every workspace is separated by a `#222` border to prevent visual bleeding while working at high zoom.
* **Mode Awareness:** The header's mode switcher (Forge, Lab, Stage) will eventually swap the contents of the **Inspector** and **Toolbox**.
* **Non-Destructive Inspector:** Sliders in the **Vibe Lab** calculate the mathematical oscillation in real-time without altering the base vector coordinates.
